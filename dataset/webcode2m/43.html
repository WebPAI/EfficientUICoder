Usedorganmusic.co.uk purchases and distributes second hand books and sheet music. It deals with printed sheet music for the organ together with books on organs and Church music; some choral music is also available.

A vast amount of stock is held from which rare and out of print items can often be found for a reasonable price. A limited amount of stock is also held which comes directly from various music publishers.

The aim of this new website is to make buying organ music simple; giving all the information needed to make an informed decision and the ability to purchase the correct item for current your needs.
