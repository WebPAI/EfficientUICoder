What is FreeLegalAdvice.org.uk ?

FreeLegalAdvice.org.uk was created to help our visitors find the best resources for free legal advice in the UK. It was specifically created to be easy to use with no sales talk or jargon - just a place to find free legal advice within a few clicks.

We list the contact details and the websites of companies who specifically offer free legal advice. We do recommend though that when you contact a company listed on our website, that you check how much free legal advice is offered by them before it starts to become chargeable.
