October 2013 Meeting

The October meeting will focus on a review of this year – both club activities and our own beekeeping experiences. Please share what went well and what could have been better. We’ll also discuss planning for next year and winter reading materials.

If you have some honey that you are willing to share with our hosts at CPI, please bring a jar to donate.

Meeting Date:

Wednesday, October 23 at 7:00

Location:

CPI
540 North Harrison Rd
Pleasant Gap, PA, 7:00 pm
Topic: Evaluation and Planning

Topic:

Evaluation and planning.

Agenda:

  • New business
  • Next meeting
  • Outreach and speaking opportunities
  • PA State Beekeepers meeting
  • Review of this year’s activities, planning for next year
  • Winter reading
  • Open discussion, question and answer session on topics raised by participants
If you have anything to add to the agenda, please let us know. If you know of anyone who might be interested in joining the Centre County Beekeepers, please encourage them to attend or contact us. The best way to reach us is by email at centrecountybeekeepers@gmail.com.
Please share this mailing with anyone that you think would be interested in attending the October meeting.
