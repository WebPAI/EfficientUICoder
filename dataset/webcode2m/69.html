北大南加州校友会2017换届选举
 亲爱的校友们：
 
大家好！今年年底我们本届执委会的任期和理事会中8位理事的任期即将结束。另有两位理事已经在年中提出辞职。根据本会的章程，我们需要在年底之前选出下届执委会，并由本届理事会选出不超过12位新任理事和任期尚未结束的7名理事一起组成下一届理事会。到11月30日提名截止日期结束时，有一组执委会竞选团队和10位理事候选人完成了提名程序。本届改选大会定于12月17号(周日)上午11点至下午3点在Santa Ana举行。
 
时间：12月17号(周日)上午11点至下午3点
地点：Lotus Hygiene Systems Inc, 1621 E St Andrew Place, Santa Ana, CA 92705
 
改选结束后我们将一起欢庆圣诞节的到来！
 
北京大学南加州校友会自从1985年成立以来，历任理事会和执委会一起为校友会的发展壮大付出了巨大的努力和贡献。感谢那些有能力也有社群服务精神的校友们的付出，我们的组织和南加州校友社区在蓬勃发展！
 
我们热烈期待校友们积极参加本次改选，并代表校友会对大家的积极参与表示衷心的感谢！12月17日我们尔湾再相聚！
 
北京大学南加州校友会2017年改选委员会
 
Dear PKU Alumni:
 
The term of our current PUAASC Executive Committee (EC) and 8 of our 15 members of the Board of Directors (BOD) will be ended at the end of this year. 2 board members resigned during this year. According to the PUAASC Bylaw, a new Executive Committee and up to 12 new board members should be elected before the beginning of year 2018 to serve the next term. The term of the ECs is 2 years and the term of the board members is 4 years. We now have 1 candidate team of EC and 10 candidates of the Board completed their nomination. The election  will be held on Dec 17, 2017 (Sunday) at the office of Lotus Hygiene Systems in Santa Ana.
 
Time: 11am to 3pm, Dec 17, 2017 (Sunday)
Address: Lotus Hygiene Systems Inc, 621 E St Andrew Place, Santa Ana, CA 92705. 
 
We will have a Christmas Party together to celebrate after the election. 
 
Ever since the inception of PUAASC in 1985, each term of BODs and ECs had worked together diligently serving PKU alumni in Southern California and contributed greatly to the prosperity of PUAASC. Thanks to those capable fellow alumni with the great spirit of community service who took the leadership to serve us, our organization and community is thriving! 
 
Your participation in the election is very important! We look forward to seeing you on Dec 17, 2017! 
 
Best regards,
 
PUAASC 2017 Election Committee
