Rhymer Joins Saint Francis Clinic Jackson as Nurse Practitioner

December 14, 2022

Saint Francis Healthcare System is pleased to announce Allison B. Rhymer, APRN, FNP-BC, has joined Saint Francis Clinic Jackson as a family nurse practitioner.

Rhymer earned her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from Murray State University in Murray, Ky., followed by her Master of Science in Nursing from Maryville University in St. Louis. Rhymer has nearly ten years of nursing experience in labor and delivery, women’s health and primary care.

Rhymer is board-certified by the American Nurses Credentialing Center.
