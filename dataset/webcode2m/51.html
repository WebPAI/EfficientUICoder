Q Clothing Closet
Thursday

The last Q Clothing Closet of the semester is coming up. The Q Closet exists to provide free clothing to trans and gender nonconforming folks, and anyone else who might need it. Stop by one of our pop-up shops to get free clothing, shoes, accessories and makeup.

Date: Thursday, Nov. 10
Time: 11 a.m. - 1 p.m.
Location: Thomas Building 110

Email Estevan Parra at estevanp@csufresno.edu with any questions.
